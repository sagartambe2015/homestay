# Konkan Village Reset — MVP PRD (v2)

## TL;DR

Build a simple, high-conversion website that sells a 
"2-Day Konkan Village Reset Experience" using strong storytelling 
and a single clear CTA: “Plan Your Stay”.

Primary goal:
→ Generate first 10–20 paying customers

Secondary goals:
→ Build trust with villagers
→ Demonstrate concept to investors

---

## Core Hypothesis

Urban users are willing to pay for:
- Peaceful, slow, non-touristy experiences
- Authentic village life
- Emotional reset from city stress

If presented clearly and credibly,
they will inquire and book via a simple flow (WhatsApp).

---

## Target User

### Primary Persona
- Age: 25–45
- Location: Mumbai / Pune
- Profile: Working professionals, couples
- Motivation:
  - Escape noise
  - Slow down
  - Experience something real

---

## Core Offering

### Product Name
2-Day Konkan Village Reset Experience

### Includes
- Stay with local family
- Home-cooked meals
- Temple visit + aarti
- Village walks
- Optional beach visit (~1 hr away)

---

## UX Strategy

The website must answer in <5 seconds:

1. What is this?
2. Why is it different?
3. What should I do next?

---

## Primary CTA

**“Plan Your Stay”**

### CTA Behavior
- Redirect to WhatsApp chat
- Pre-filled message:
  “Hi, I’m interested in the Konkan Village Reset experience”

---

## Page Structure (Conversion Flow)

### 1. Hero Section (Hook)

Headline:
Escape the Noise. Experience Real Konkan Village Life.

Subtext:
Stay with a local family, enjoy home-cooked meals, 
visit a peaceful temple, and truly slow down.

CTA:
Plan Your Stay

Support line:
2-Day Village Reset • Limited slots

---

### 2. Problem Section (Relatability)

Tired of crowded, noisy vacations?

Most trips today feel rushed and commercial.
This experience is designed for people who want to slow down.

---

### 3. Solution Section (Clarity)

A Simple Village Experience

- Stay in a real Konkan home
- Eat traditional home-cooked meals
- Visit a peaceful local temple
- Enjoy quiet village life

CTA:
Check Availability

---

### 4. Experience Section (Tangibility)

Your 2-Day Reset

Day 1:
Arrival, village walk, home-cooked dinner

Day 2:
Temple visit, relaxed day, optional beach trip

Day 3:
Breakfast and departure

---

### 5. Trust Section

Testimonials (even 2–3)

Example:
“Very peaceful and authentic experience”
“Felt like home, amazing food”

---

### 6. Host Credibility

Line:
Hosted by local families in our native village in Konkan.

(Optional)
Show 1–2 home images

---

### 7. Final CTA Section

Headline:
Ready to take a break from city life?

CTA:
Plan Your Stay

Support line:
Small groups only. Limited availability.

---

## Booking Flow

1. User clicks CTA
2. Opens WhatsApp
3. Sends pre-filled message
4. Manual follow-up:
   - Dates
   - Group size
   - Pricing
5. Payment via UPI

---

## Product Principles

- One clear action > many options
- Emotion > information
- Simplicity > completeness
- Real > polished

---

## What We Are NOT Building

- Marketplace
- Payment gateway
- User accounts
- Complex backend

---

## Success Metrics

### Primary
- # of CTA clicks
- # of WhatsApp inquiries
- # of bookings

### Secondary
- Conversion rate (visit → inquiry)
- Referral rate
- Guest satisfaction

---

## Risks

### 1. Weak positioning
Mitigation:
Strong, emotional copy

---

### 2. No trust signals
Mitigation:
Photos, testimonials, host visibility

---

### 3. No clear action
Mitigation:
CTA repeated across page
